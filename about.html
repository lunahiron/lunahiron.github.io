Luna is a Ph.D. student in Meteorology and Physical Oceanography at the Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Sciences (RSMAS), University of Miami. She works at the Upper Ocean Dynamics Lab under Prof. Nick Shay and is studying the Loop Current System and the role of the frontal eddies in the separation dynamic through theory, in situ data, and numerical models. Luna is interested in large and mesoscale ocean dynamics, cross-scale interactions and energy transfer, and their impact on the climate.

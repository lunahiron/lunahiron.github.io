I am a Ph.D. candidate in Meteorology and Physical Oceanography at the Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Sciences (RSMAS), University of Miami. I work in the Upper Ocean Dynamics Lab under Prof. Nick Shay, and my research focuses on the dynamics of the Loop Current and its frontal eddies in the Gulf of Mexico using theory, observations, and numerical models. I am interested in large and mesoscale ocean dynamics, cross-scale interactions and energy transfer, and their impact on the climate system.
